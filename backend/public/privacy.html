Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

Riffly is a Chrome extension that drafts cold LinkedIn messages. Here's what we keep when you use it, what we don't, and how to get rid of any of it.

Data We Collect

When you use Riffly:

  • LinkedIn profile content. The headline, bio, about section, and recent post you've got open when you click Riffly get sent to our backend so the model can draft something specific. We don't keep them past the 30-day debug window described below.
  • Account email. Used to identify your account for billing, quota, and login.
  • Drafts you generated. Stored against your account so you've got a record of what was sent.
  • Usage logs. A row per generation: who, when, which plan. Used for billing and to debug pipeline issues.

Google User Data (Sign in with Google)

If you sign in with Google, the bullets below are the whole story. Riffly's handling of Google user data follows the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use rules.

What we receive

Three non-sensitive OAuth scopes:

  • openid — proves the account is real.
  • email — your primary Google email.
  • profile — your display name and your avatar URL if you've set one.

That's the full list. We don't ask for Gmail. We don't touch Drive, Calendar, Contacts, YouTube, your location, or any other Google service. No restricted or sensitive scopes, ever.

What we do with it

  • Account identifier. Your Google email is the key we use to find your Riffly account when you come back.
  • Sign-in. Google's OAuth tokens tell us it's still you on subsequent visits. We never see your Google password.
  • Showing you who you're signed in as. Your name and avatar appear in your own dashboard so you can confirm the right account is active.

We do not use Google user data for advertising, profiling, training general AI models, or selling or transferring it to third parties. The Limited Use rule, in plain English: we use it to log you in, and for nothing else.

Where it lives

In our Supabase auth database, US region, encrypted at rest. The OAuth refresh token is encrypted in transit and at rest. We don't copy any of this into other systems beyond what's needed to keep you signed in.

How long we keep it

For as long as your Riffly account exists. Email support@rifflylabs.com to delete your account, and we erase it within 30 days (minus what we're legally required to retain for billing). You can also revoke Riffly's access from Google's side at any time at myaccount.google.com/permissions.

Who else sees it

Only Supabase, our auth sub-processor, and only because they're hosting the session. Anthropic doesn't see it. Stripe doesn't see it. Resend doesn't see it. We don't sell, rent, or trade Google user data.

Limited Use compliance

Riffly's use of Google user data is limited to user-facing features that are visible and obvious in the app: signing you in, identifying your account, and showing your name and avatar in your own dashboard. We don't transfer this data except to provide or improve those features, to comply with applicable law, or as part of a sale or merger (where the new owner is bound by this same policy). We don't use it for advertising. Humans on our side only look at this data with your explicit consent on a specific issue, when investigating abuse for security reasons, when required by law, or as aggregated stats — per the Google API Services User Data Policy.

What We Don't Collect

  • We don't crawl LinkedIn. Riffly reads the profile that's currently in front of you, only when you click the icon.
  • No third-party cookies, no cross-site tracking.
  • No background traversal of your connections, your feed, or anything else on LinkedIn.
  • Your LinkedIn password and session cookies stay where they belong: in your browser. We never see them.
  • We don't keep tabs on which profiles you visit or how you use LinkedIn outside of when you click Riffly.

Sub-Processors

Five vendors touch your data, each with a specific job. All five are bound by data processing agreements that meet the GDPR Article 28 bar:

  • Anthropic (US) — receives the profile snapshot and your pitch so Claude can draft. This is the core of the product. Privacy policy.
  • Stripe (US) — handles your email and card for billing. Privacy policy.
  • Supabase (US) — the Postgres database and auth layer. Privacy policy.
  • Vercel (US) — hosts the website and the API routes; also rate-limits abuse. Privacy policy.
  • Resend (US) — sends our transactional emails (digests, install reminders). Privacy policy.

That's the full list. No ad networks, no analytics resellers, no data brokers. We don't sell, rent, or share your data for any marketing or advertising purpose.

Data Retention

Profile snapshots and the drafts we generated for you stick around for 30 days, mostly so we can debug specific issues you might raise with support. After that they're deleted automatically. Account and billing records are kept for the life of your subscription, or however long the law requires.

Security

HTTPS everywhere — between the extension and our backend, between us and our sub-processors. Storage is encrypted at rest. Access is locked down to the team members who actually need it.

Your Rights

No matter where you live, you can:

  • see what we have on you;
  • correct anything that's wrong;
  • have your account and data deleted;
  • restrict or object to specific kinds of processing;
  • get a portable copy of your data in a machine-readable format;
  • withdraw consent where consent was the basis for what we were doing.

Email support@rifflylabs.com to do any of those. We turn around requests inside 30 days.

For EU, UK, and EEA Users (GDPR)

Data Controller. Riffly Labs is the data controller for personal data processed through rifflylabs.com and the Riffly Chrome extension. Reach us at support@rifflylabs.com.

Legal Bases for Processing (Article 6 GDPR).

  • Performance of a contract: providing the extension and dashboard you signed up for, including drafting, account management, and billing.
  • Legitimate interests: operating, securing, and improving the service. That covers rate limiting, abuse prevention, and debugging. You can object to processing under this basis.
  • Compliance with legal obligations: tax, accounting, and any disclosures required of us.

International Data Transfers. Our infrastructure vendors (Anthropic, Stripe, Vercel, Supabase, Resend) are based in the United States. Personal data goes to the US under safeguards that include the EU-US Data Privacy Framework, where the recipient is certified, and Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission.

Right to Lodge a Complaint. If you think we've processed your data unlawfully, you can complain to your local supervisory authority. The European Data Protection Board keeps a directory at edpb.europa.eu. UK residents go to the Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk.

Automated Decision-Making. Riffly uses an LLM to draft outreach text on your behalf. That's automated content generation, not automated decision-making with legal or similarly significant effects. You read every draft and decide whether to send it.

If You Are a Recruiter Using Riffly to Process Candidate Data (Article 14 GDPR)

If you're using Riffly to draft outreach to candidates, you (or your employer) are the data controller for those candidates' personal data under GDPR. Riffly is the processor handling it on your behalf. Article 14 says the controller has to tell the candidate about the processing within a reasonable period. That notification obligation is yours, not ours. If your jurisdiction or your employer requires it, a one-liner in your outreach is usually enough, something like: "I drafted this with a tool called Riffly that processed your public LinkedIn profile content. Reply STOP and I'll take you off any future outreach." A standard Data Processing Agreement covering Riffly's processor role is available on request at support@rifflylabs.com.

For California Users (CCPA / CPRA)

California residents can ask what personal info we have, get it deleted, correct what's wrong, opt out of any sale or sharing, and not be punished for asking. We don't sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. Email support@rifflylabs.com to use any of those rights.

Children

Riffly isn't aimed at anyone under 16. We don't knowingly collect data from kids. If you think we have, tell us and we'll delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We update this policy when something material changes. When that happens we email everyone with an account.
