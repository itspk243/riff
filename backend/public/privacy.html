Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Riffly is a Chrome extension that drafts personalized LinkedIn cold messages. This privacy policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

Data We Collect

When you use Riffly, we collect the following data:

  • LinkedIn Profile Data: The profile information you paste into the Riffly popup (headline, bio, about section, recent posts) is sent to our backend to generate a draft message.
  • Account Email: Your email address is stored to identify your account for billing and quota management.
  • Generated Messages: The AI-drafted messages you generate for your records and auditing purposes.
  • Usage Logs: We log that a generation event occurred, your account ID, and timestamp for billing and feature tracking.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not automatically scrape LinkedIn. Riffly only reads what's visible on the profile page you're viewing.
  • We do not use cookies to track you across websites.
  • We do not crawl your LinkedIn connections or activity feed in the background.
  • We do not store your LinkedIn password or authentication tokens.
  • We do not monitor which profiles you visit or how you use LinkedIn.

Sub-Processors

Your data is shared only with the following sub-processors, each for a specific purpose. All are bound by data processing agreements that meet GDPR Article 28 requirements:

  • Anthropic (US) — profile data and your pitch description are sent to Claude for message generation. Required for the core product. Privacy policy.
  • Stripe (US) — email and billing information for payment processing and subscription management. Privacy policy.
  • Supabase (US) — primary application database (Postgres) and authentication. Privacy policy.
  • Vercel (US) — web hosting, edge functions, and rate limiting. Privacy policy.
  • Resend (US) — transactional email delivery (digest emails, install reminders). Privacy policy.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any other parties for marketing, advertising, or other purposes.

Data Retention

We retain profile data and generated messages for 30 days to support customer debugging and feature improvement. After 30 days, this data is automatically deleted. Account and billing data are retained as long as your subscription remains active or as required by law.

Security

All data transmission between the Riffly extension and our backend is encrypted using HTTPS. We follow industry best practices for data storage and access control.

Your Rights

Regardless of where you live, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Restrict or object to certain processing
  • Receive a copy of your data in a portable, machine-readable format
  • Withdraw consent at any time, where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, email support@rifflylabs.com. We respond within 30 days.

For EU, UK, and EEA Users (GDPR)

Data Controller. Riffly Labs is the data controller for personal data processed through rifflylabs.com and the Riffly Chrome extension. Contact: support@rifflylabs.com.

Legal Bases for Processing (Article 6 GDPR).

  • Performance of a contract — to provide the extension and dashboard you signed up for (profile drafting, account management, billing).
  • Legitimate interests — to operate, secure, and improve the service (rate limiting, abuse prevention, debugging). You may object to processing under this basis.
  • Compliance with legal obligations — for tax, accounting, and any required disclosures.

International Data Transfers. Our infrastructure providers (Anthropic, Stripe, Vercel, Supabase, Resend) are primarily based in the United States. Personal data is transferred to the US under safeguards that include the EU-US Data Privacy Framework (where the recipient is certified) and Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission.

Right to Lodge a Complaint. If you believe we have processed your data unlawfully, you can complain to your local supervisory authority. A directory is maintained by the European Data Protection Board at edpb.europa.eu. UK residents may complain to the Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk.

Automated Decision-Making. Riffly uses an LLM to draft outreach text on your behalf. This is automated content generation, not automated decision-making with legal or similarly significant effects. You always review and decide whether to send the draft.

If You Are a Recruiter Using Riffly to Process Candidate Data (Article 14 GDPR)

If you are a recruiter, sourcer, or talent professional using Riffly to draft outreach to candidates, you (or your employer) are the data controller for the candidate's personal data under GDPR. Riffly processes that data on your behalf as a processor. Article 14 of GDPR generally requires the controller to inform the data subject (the candidate) about the processing within a reasonable period. That notification obligation is yours, not ours. We strongly encourage including a one-line note in your outreach (for example: "I drafted this message using a tool called Riffly which processed your public LinkedIn profile content. Reply STOP and I'll remove you from any future outreach.") if your jurisdiction or your employer requires it. A standard Data Processing Agreement covering Riffly's role as your processor is available at support@rifflylabs.com.

For California Users (CCPA / CPRA)

California residents have the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion, correct inaccurate information, opt out of sale or sharing of personal information, and not be discriminated against for exercising these rights. We do not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. To exercise your rights, email support@rifflylabs.com.

Children

Riffly is not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have, contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email to your registered account address.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy or our data practices, contact us at support@rifflylabs.com.
