Typography

Inter at font-weight: 600 with letter-spacing: -0.02em for the wordmark and headings. Body type uses the system stack at 400/500.

Usage

Do
  • Keep clear space around the mark equal to one bar's height.
  • Use the SVG version whenever possible — it scales without softening.
  • Pair with the warm #faf9f6 background or the ink #111110 background.
  • Use the accent variant sparingly — typically once per surface.
Don't
  • Don't recolor the bars outside the palette above.
  • Don't add gradients, drop shadows, or strokes.
  • Don't stretch the mark non-proportionally.
  • Don't place the mark on a busy photo or low-contrast background.
  • Don't typeset the wordmark in a different typeface.

Naming
