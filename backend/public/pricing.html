Pricing questions

What happens when I hit my draft cap?

The popup tells you you're at the limit. Free users can share a roast at rifflylabs.com/roast for a one-time +3 bonus, or upgrade to Pro. Pro and Plus users either wait for the calendar reset or upgrade to the next tier.

Can I switch plans?

Anytime, both directions. Upgrades take effect immediately and we prorate the difference. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period — you keep your current tier until then.

Do you offer team or agency pricing?

Not yet. If you're 5+ recruiters and want a single bill, email support@rifflylabs.com — we'll work something out manually until the team plan ships.

Is there a free trial of Pro or Plus?

The free tier is the trial. 3 drafts a week is enough to send to a handful of real candidates and decide whether the replies you're getting back are different. After that, Pro is $15/month with a 14-day refund window if you've used fewer than 50 generations.

What payment methods do you accept?

All major credit and debit cards, plus Apple Pay and Google Pay through Stripe. We don't currently accept ACH, wire, or invoice billing.

Do you charge tax / VAT?

Stripe Tax handles this automatically based on your billing address. EU VAT-registered businesses can enter their VAT number at checkout to avoid being charged VAT.

Where can I see my invoices?

The dashboard's billing section opens the Stripe customer portal, where you can download every invoice, update your card, and cancel.

What's actually different about Plus vs Pro?

Three things: 600 drafts/month instead of 200; live fit-scoring against your saved job specs (Active Profile Assist); and a daily email digest of the top candidates from your saved LinkedIn searches. If you scan saved searches more than weekly or have multiple active roles, Plus pays back. If you mostly work one-candidate-at-a-time, Pro is plenty.
